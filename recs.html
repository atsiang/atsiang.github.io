comics

  • Runaways
    Teenagers vs their supervillain parents ! One of my all-time favourites, (don't watch the show, it is trash), the reason I started reading comics again.
  • Paper Girls
    The time-travel adventures of 4 pre-teen girls! A+ for Matthew Wilson's coloring, it really makes the art stand out.
  • Descender
    An epic tale of a young android caught in the middle of a robot genocide, all told through WATERCOLORS.
  • Die
    Okay it's a little depressing but Stephanie hans' art is amazing! Gillen's notes on worldbuilding, characters, etc are just as interesting as the actual book (maybe it's designer in me who just loves to hear about process lol).
